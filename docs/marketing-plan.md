# MyCrew Marketing Plan

## Executive Summary
MyCrew is a creator monetization platform competing with OnlyFans, Fanvue, and Fansly. Our key differentiators: 10% platform fee (vs 20%), 90-day free trial, anti-screenshot protection, and collaboration marketplace.

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Months -2 to 0)

### Goals
- 500 waitlist signups
- 50 beta creators committed
- Brand awareness in creator communities

### Tactics

#### 1. Creator Outreach (High Priority)
**Target**: Micro-influencers (10K-100K followers) on Instagram, TikTok, Twitter
- DM campaign: 100 creators/day
- Offer: Founding Creator status (lifetime 8% fee instead of 10%)
- Focus on niches: Fitness, Fashion, Art, Gaming, Adult

**Script**:
```
Hey [Name], love your content! Building MyCrew - creator platform 
with 10% fees (vs 20% on OF). 90-day free trial + collab marketplace.

Want founding creator status? 8% lifetime fee.

[Calendly link for 15-min call]
```

#### 2. Reddit Marketing
**Subreddits**: r/onlyfansadvice, r/creatorsadvice, r/sexworkers, r/beermoney
- Value-first posts: "How I saved $2K in fees switching platforms"
- AMAs: "Built a creator platform, ask me anything"
- No direct promotion - build trust first

#### 3. Twitter/X Growth
- Daily threads on creator economy
- Engage with creator complaints about OF
- Share behind-the-scenes build journey
- Target: 5,000 followers by launch

#### 4. Discord Communities
- Join 20+ creator Discord servers
- Be helpful, answer questions
- Soft pitch when relevant
- Create MyCrew Discord for beta testers

## Phase 2: Launch (Month 1)

### Goals
- 200 active creators
- 5,000 users registered
- $10,000 GMV (Gross Merchandise Value)

### Tactics

#### 1. Launch Week Blast
- Product Hunt launch
- Indie Hackers post
- Hacker News Show HN
- Twitter Spaces with beta creators

#### 2. Paid Ads (Small Budget)
**Budget**: $2,000
- Instagram Stories: $1,000 (target: creators, 18-35)
- TikTok: $500 (trending sounds, platform comparisons)
- Twitter: $500 (promoted tweets in creator threads)

#### 3. Influencer Partnerships
- 10 micro-influencers (10K-50K)
- Offer: $500 + revenue share for 3 posts
- Content: Platform walkthrough, fee comparison, earnings reveal

#### 4. Referral Program (Pre-Rising Star)
- Creators get $10 per referred creator who makes $100
- Track in background for Rising Star prep

## Phase 3: Growth (Months 2-6)

### Goals
- 1,000 active creators
- 50,000 users
- $100,000 GMV/month
- 20% MoM growth

### Tactics

#### 1. Content Marketing
**Blog Topics**:
- "How to make $10K/month as a creator"
- "Platform fee comparison: OF vs MyCrew vs Fansly"
- "Tax guide for content creators"
- "Collaboration strategies for growth"

**SEO**: Target "onlyfans alternative", "creator platform", "monetize content"

#### 2. YouTube Channel
- Weekly creator interviews
- Platform tutorials
- Earnings breakdowns (with creators)
- Target: 10K subscribers by month 6

#### 3. Podcast Sponsorships
- Creator economy podcasts
- Adult industry podcasts
- Budget: $3,000/month

#### 4. Email Marketing
- Weekly newsletter: "The Creator Economy"
- Tips, trends, success stories
- Platform updates
- Target: 50K subscribers by month 6

#### 5. Viral Marketing
**Challenges**:
- #MyCrewChallenge (creator transitions)
- Fee savings calculator (shareable)
- "How much I saved" testimonials

## Phase 4: Scale (Months 6-12)

### Goals
- 5,000 active creators
- 500,000 users
- $1M GMV/month
- Launch Rising Star bonus program

### Tactics

#### 1. Rising Star Program Launch
- $500 top prize monthly
- Heavy promotion: "Get paid to grow"
- PR push: "Creator platform pays users to join"

#### 2. Strategic Partnerships
- Adult toy companies (affiliate deals)
- Payment processors (co-marketing)
- VPN services (privacy-focused creators)

#### 3. Events
- Sponsor creator conventions
- Host virtual summits
- Meetups in major cities

#### 4. International Expansion
- EU launch (GDPR compliant)
- LATAM (Spanish/Portuguese)
- Asia (localized payment methods)

## Channel Strategy

### Organic (60% of effort)
- Twitter/X: Daily engagement
- Reddit: 5 value posts/week
- Discord: Community building
- SEO: Long-term traffic

### Paid (30% of effort)
- Instagram/TikTok: Creator acquisition
- Google Ads: Search intent
- Podcasts: Trust building

### Partnerships (10% of effort)
- Influencer collabs
- Affiliate program
- Strategic integrations

## Risk Mitigation

### Platform Risk
- Multi-cloud setup
- Backup payment processors
- Content moderation team

### Competition
- Faster feature development
- Better creator support
- Lower fees locked in contract

### Regulatory
- Age verification strict
- Compliance team
- Legal counsel on retainer
