# MyCrew Lean Marketing Plan (Bootstrap)

## Philosophy
Zero paid ads. 100% organic growth. Leverage Brooklynn East's existing audience and network.

## Budget: $0-500/Month

## Phase 1: Soft Launch (Month 1-2)

### 1. Brooklynn East Network (Primary Channel)
**Your Assets:**
- Instagram, TikTok, X, OnlyFans followers
- Existing creator relationships
- AI influencer automation system

**Tactics:**
- Weekly "Behind the Business" content showing MyCrew build
- Tease platform features in Stories
- DM top 50 creators personally (1:1 outreach)
- Offer: "Founding 100" get lifetime 8% fee

**Time Investment:** 5 hrs/week
**Cost:** $0

### 2. Creator-to-Creator Referral
**The Hook:**
"I'm building this for us. Help me test it?"

**Script:**
```
Hey [name], been following your content. Building a creator 
platform with lower fees (10% vs 20%). Want early access?

No pressure - just need feedback from creators I respect.

-Brooklynn
```

**Target:** 5 new creators/week
**Conversion:** 20% = 1 creator/week
**Year 1:** 50 creators from direct outreach

### 3. Reddit (Free Traffic)
**Subreddits:**
- r/onlyfansadvice (200K members)
- r/creatorsadvice (50K members)
- r/beermoney (700K members)

**Strategy:**
- Value-first posts only
- "How I reduced platform fees by 50%"
- Share revenue comparisons
- Answer questions, build reputation
- Soft mention: "Built my own solution"

**Time:** 2 hrs/week
**Cost:** $0

### 4. Twitter/X Growth
**Content Calendar:**
- Monday: Platform fee comparison
- Wednesday: Creator success story
- Friday: Behind-the-scenes build

**Engagement:**
- Reply to creator complaints about OF
- Quote tweet with helpful advice
- Build in public (#buildinpublic)

**Time:** 3 hrs/week
**Cost:** $0

## Phase 2: Organic Growth (Month 3-6)

### 1. Creator Testimonials
**Ask early users:**

- Video testimonial (30 sec)
- "Why I switched" blog post

**Incentive:** Free month of platform fees

### 2. Collaboration Cross-Promotion
**Partner with:**
- 5 micro-influencers (10K-50K)

- No cash exchanged

### 3. Discord Community
**Create:** MyCrew Creators Discord
- Free tips and advice

### 4. SEO Content
**Blog posts (you write):**
- "OnlyFans fee calculator"
- "Creator platform comparison 2024"
- "How to make $10K/month as a creator"

**Publish on:**
- Medium (free)

**Time:** 4 hrs/week
**Cost:** $0

## Phase 3: Network Effects (Month 6-12)

### 1. Rising Star Program Launch
**Now you have revenue to fund it:**
- $500/month top prize

**Creators earn:**

### 3. Case Studies
**Document success stories:**
- "How [Creator] saved $2,400 in fees"
