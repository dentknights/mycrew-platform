### Key Variables
1. **Creator acquisition cost**: ±$10 = ±$34K impact
2. **Churn rate**: ±5% = ±$50K impact
3. **Average earnings**: ±$500 = ±$150K impact

## Risk Factors

### High Impact, High Probability
- **Payment processor shutdown**: Mitigate with 3+ processors
- **Chargeback fraud**: 1% reserve, strict verification

### High Impact, Low Probability
- **Regulatory changes**: Legal compliance team
- **Major competitor price war**: Lock in low fees contractually

### Low Impact, High Probability
- **Server outages**: Multi-region redundancy
- **Creator disputes**: 24/7 support team

## Exit Strategy

### Acquisition Targets
- **OnlyFans**: Strategic (eliminate competitor)
- **Fanvue**: Consolidation play
- **Adult industry conglomerates**: Vertical integration

### Valuation Multiples
- **Current market**: 3-5x revenue
- **High growth**: 5-10x revenue
- **MyCrew target**: 8x revenue (Month 24)

### Potential Exit Values
- **Year 2**: $15M (8x $1.87M)
- **Year 3**: $40M (8x $5M)

## Conclusion

MyCrew projects strong unit economics with 75:1 LTV:CAC ratio and 90% gross margins. The platform reaches profitability by Month 3 and scales to $20M+ net income by Year 5. Key success factors: maintaining low CAC, minimizing churn, and scaling infrastructure efficiently.

**Recommendation**: Bootstrap with $10-50K initial investment, raise seed at Month 6-12 based on traction.
