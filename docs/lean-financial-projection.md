## Funding Decision Matrix

### Bootstrap (Recommended for You)
**Pros:**
- 100% ownership
- No debt
- Full control
- Learn the business

**Cons:**
- Slower growth
- More time investment
- Less margin for error

**Best if:** You have time, existing audience, low living costs

### Seed Funding
**Pros:**
- Faster growth
- Can hire team
- Professional network

**Cons:**
- Dilution
- Reporting obligations
- Pressure to scale

**Best if:** You want to dominate market, have high living costs

## Lean Success Factors

### Must-Have (Non-Negotiable)
1. **19 hrs/week commitment**
2. **Consistent Brooklynn content**
3. **Daily creator outreach**
4. **Zero paid ads discipline**

### Nice-to-Have
1. Part-time VA ($300/month at Month 6)

3. Rising Star program ($2,725/month at Month 6)

### Kill-the-Business Risks
1. Inconsistent effort
2. Ignoring customer feedback
3. Adding features instead of marketing
4. Spending on ads too early
